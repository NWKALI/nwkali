Your Instructor

Lineage Matters. So Does 37 Years of Application.

An instructor in the Inosanto method of FMA / JKD since 1987, Sifu/Guro Clarke opened the first Kali / JKD school in Portland, OR under the Inosanto lineage — and the first commercial Muay Thai school in Portland. In 1998 Sifu Clarke was awarded the rank of Full Instructor under Master Inosanto. For decades he has assisted in teaching all levels of law enforcement throughout Oregon, Washington, and California.
